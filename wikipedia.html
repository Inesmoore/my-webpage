Rebecca Enonchong

Rebecca Enonchong FREng [born in july 14 [2] 1967] is a Cameroonian technology entrepreneur and also the founder and CEO of Apptech.She is best known for her work promoting technology in Africa

Enonchang has been a recipient of various awards from organizationssuch as the listed her as one of the 10 Female Tech Founders To Watch in African during 2014

Early life and Education [edit]

Enonchang ws born in the Southwest Region of Camerron in 1967.Her father was Dr. Henry Ndifor Abi Enonghang Barrister in Cameroon who was a well known Barrister in Cameroon .While Enonchang was growing up in Cameroon her father helped create the Federal Cameroon Bar Association and its succesor.The Cameroon Bar Association,[4]

In her teens ,Enonghang moved to the US with the family[5] While studying.,she took up a selling door-to-door newspaper subscription from the age of 15 .She later became a manager at the same company at the age of 17

Enonchang attended the Catholic University of America Where she graduated with a Bachekor of science degree and also a Master of sciencedegree in Economics

Career

After finishing her eduvation,Enonchong went on to work for a number oforganizations including Inter -American Development Bank(laDB) and Oracle [8]

In 1999,Enonghong founded the company Apptech ,a bethesda,Maryland-based global provider of enterprize application solutions.

Appstech is an Oracle platinium Partnerand has customers in over 40countries

AppsTech opened offices in other countries including Enonchong native cameroon She describes the experience as having been difficult and having led to the closure of AppsTech Subsidiaris


In 2002 ,The world economic forum of Davos Switzerlandnamed Enonchonga Global leather for Tomorrow[GLT] along with other tech entrepreneurs such as Googleco-founder Larry Pageand Salesforce.comCEO Marc Beniof

In 2013 Enonchong was recognized as a finalist for Africa digital woman award .In march 2014 Forbeslisted her as one of the ' 10 Female Tech Founders to Watch in Africa

In August 2021 ,Rebecca Enonchong was arrested and taken into custody in Douala .Her lawyers denounce a 'serious abuse of authority'on the part of the judicial system


Non profit work

Enonchong has spent much of her career promoting technology in Africa .She has carried out the work in both US and Africa.She was the founder and chair person of African Technology Forum ,a non profit dedicated to helping technology startups in Africa


Enonchong is a member of te board of directors for the Foundation [17].She is a member of the UK Department for International Development's Digital Advisory Panel ,[19] and was previously involved with the UN,s Women Global Advisory Commitment and the United Nations ICT Task Force
